About Alard College

Alard College of Engineering and Management, Pune is one of the premier institutes under Alard Group of Institutions. It offers UG and PG programs in various engineering disciplines, management, and computer applications. The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE. It emphasizes quality education, innovation, and holistic development.

Campus Placement Drives

The college conducts regular campus recruitment drives with participation from top companies such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Capgemini, Cognizant, and many more. Soft skills and aptitude training sessions are organized throughout the year to prepare students for placement.

Facilities at a Glance

The campus includes well-equipped labs, digital classrooms, a central library, sports grounds, hostel facilities, cafeteria, and a digital campus. It provides a perfect balance of academics and extracurriculars.
